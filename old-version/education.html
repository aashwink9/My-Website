Educational Background

Being born in India, I completed my education till highschool in my hometown. I live in a city called Raipur, Chhattisgarh. I was admitted to many schools before I reached the 5th grade, where I completed my school education in the same school.

I started my education from a school called Angel's Nursery in my hometown Raipur, where I completed my preschool, and then when my parents moved to another neighbouring city, I got admitted to another school called St. Xaviers, where I completed my Kindergarden. After my parents moved back to Raipur, I got admitted to a different school called Holy Hearts Educational Academy, where I was only for the duration of two years: the fourth grade and the fifth grade. In the middle of completing the fifth grade, I finally moved to N.H. Goel World School in my hometown, where I completed the rest of my education. Starting from the seventh grade (middle school), I realized my passion for computers, however I was also active in a variety of clubs, like the sports soccer club, the chess club and the Music club where I started to learn to play a guitar.

In the 11th grade (highschool), when we were finally allowed to choose our desired subjects, I chose a Computer Science and Science bundle where the Science section included Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. I started learning more and more about computers, and started to get more into video games as well. It was after completing my 12th grade that I realized I wanted to go to the United States and pursue my education in computer science, as it had a variety of different fields and combinations that one wouldn't find in colleges in India. So that is my educational story, and moving forward, I hope I am able to learn more in this field and do something that would contribute to the world in a major, impactful way.
